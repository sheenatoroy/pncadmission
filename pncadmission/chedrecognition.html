CHED Recognition

Pamantasan ng Cabuyao was awarded a “GRANT OF RECOGNITION” as a higher education institution by the Commission on Higher Education (CHED) last 18th day of May 2017 at UP Diliman, Quezon City.

The certificate was awarded by CHED Chairperson Dr. Patricia B. Licuanan to Pamantasan ng Cabuyao representatives headed by City Mayor Atty. Rommel A. Gecolea. This grant conferred to by CHED is the fruit of the city’s hard work and a reflection of the city mayor’s vision to bring out the best in every youth by providing them opportunities and access to quality tertiary education

Grant of recognition is awarded to schools that meet requirements given by CHED. “Sa dami ng local colleges sa bansa, Pamantasan ng Cabuyao is a very good example to other local universities and we always want to ensure the quality of instruction. PnC did it we well and so we are recognizing them as a higher education institution. Congratulations for a job well done.” said CHED Chairperson Dr. Patricia Licuanan.
